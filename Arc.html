Anti Ragging Cell

As per orders of Hon’ble High Court, Hon’ble Supreme Court and the rules framed by the Himachal Pradesh University vide ordinance 22.17, Ragging is strictly prohibited. Any student found involved in Ragging will be summarily expelled from the college and legal proceedings under Criminal Law shall be initiated against him/her.
